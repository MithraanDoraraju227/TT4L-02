About Us

MMU Echo is your platform to voice your thoughts and feedback about various aspects of university life in MMU. Whether it's about a recent lecture, campus facilities, or general suggestions, this is the place where your opinions matter. Leave us comments on specific topics or general feedback. You can also view comments from other students to see how their experiences compare to yours. It's a great way to gauge the overall sentiment and find common areas of interest or concern. Use our easy to use dropdown menu to filter comments by topic.
